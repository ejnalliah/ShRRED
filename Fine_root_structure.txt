***Fine-root structure Protocol*** (Adopted from Weemstra et al. 2020) 

Materials: 

Metal corer
Sledgehammer 
Plastic bags
Sharpie 
DI water 
Trays 
Scanner 
Scale 
Drying oven 
Sieve


Protocol: 

1. Take 3 soil core to 10cm 0.5m away from the center tree and place it in labeled sampling bag

2. Sieve away soil, rocks and debris with 2mm sieve

3. Clean roots thoroughly with DI water 

4. Scan roots with WhinRhizo 

5. Dry roots at 60 degrees C for 48hrs in drying oven 

6. Weight Dried roots 

7. Determine Ash free weight
